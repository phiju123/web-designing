About Us

Vision Statement | Mission Statement


  • The undergraduate degree in Computer Engineering at S V National Institute of Technology, has been offered since 1988-89, with the first batch of Bachelors of Computer Engineering graduating in 1992 (intake 30) under the patronage of the erstwhile Department of Electronics Engineering. The Department of Computer Engineering was bifurcated from the Department of Electronics Engineering in 2002. With a focus on balancing high-quality research to substantiate the class-room teaching the Ph D program has been started in the year 2005 and the inception of the M Tech course was taken place in 2006. The department has also started the PG progamme, M.Tech. (by Research) in the year 2007. Currently, it offers the UG, PG and PhD degrees in Computer Engineering.

  • The total intake in the department at the UG level is 93 students whereas at the PG level is 25. There are in all 30 PhD scholars registered currently in the department with 04 as full-time, 01 scholar as part-time project fellow, 16 as part-time external, 06 as part-time (internal faculty members), and 03 as part-time (Lecturers on-contract basis).

  • The Computer Engineering Department has sanctioned faculty strength of 2 Professors, 3 Associate Professors, 6 Assistant Professors and 3 Lecturers on Contract. However, the deficit in the human resources is attempted to be made up by hiring teaching assistants.

  • The department has well-equipped laboratories consisting of the state-of-the-art servers, computers, microprocessor kits, embedded evaluation boards, network simulator kits, and different simulation software packages. The hardware in the laboratories consists of 18 nos of IBM/HCL servers, 182 nos of desktop PCs, 01 GPU workstation and sufficient numbers of other peripherals including printers. The department also has Wireless Sensor Network kits consisting of a variety of sensor motes viz. Mica2, Iris and Lotus motes with a range of different sensors and classroom kits.

  • The curriculum has been designed keeping in view industrial and research needs, as well as the rapid development in the IT industry. Induction of new courses from time to time enables the students to keep abreast of recent technological development. Flexibility in curriculum is provided through Electives, Seminars and Projects.

  • The students with their innovative ideas implemented, routinely present Technical Papers at National level Symposiums, and achieve laurels. The Department has always strived for excellence and receives grants to develop and modernize its laboratories. A dedicated laboratory funded by MHRD under the National Programme on Technology Enhanced Learning (NPTEL) has also been established for the purpose of E-learning through online Web and Video courses in Engineering.

Vision Statement

The vision of the Computer Engineering Department is to recognize itself as renowned department in the field of technical education in Computer Engineering and strives to carry out the superior level of research based on the quality, innovation and excellence; with the help of its stakeholders viz. the students, research scholars, faculty members, the support staff and the alumni.
